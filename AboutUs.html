About Us!

With MeloVibe, it`s easy to find the right music or podcast for every moment on your phone, your computer, your tablet and more.
There are millions of tracks and episodes on MeloVibe. So whether you`re behind the wheel, working out, partying or relaxing, the right music or podcast is always at your fingertips. Choose what you want to listen to, or let MeloVibe surprise you.

Coustomer Service and Support!

Help Site:

Check out our help site for answers to your questions and to learn how to get the most out of MeloVibe and your music.

Community:

Get fast support from expert MeloVibe users. If there isn`t already an answer there to your question, post it and someone will quickly answer. You can also suggest and vote on new ideas for MeloVibe or simply discuss music with other fans.

Contact Us:

Contact our Customer Support if you don`t find a solution on our support site or Community.
